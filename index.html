Principle Investigator (PI)



    WEN Liping

    WEN Liping , male, a Man people, a member of the Communist Party of China, is currently a researcher doctoral supervisor at the Technical Institute of Physics and Chemistry of CAS, and a professor at the School of Future Technology of CAS. He was awarded the National Outstanding Youth Fund in 2016. His representative publications include 4 papers on Angew. Chem. Int. Ed., 2 on J. Am. Chem. Soc. , 10 on Adv. Mater. and 1on Science Advances. He has instructed students to receive scholarships several times, including “National Scholarships”, “Zhu Liyuehua Scholarship”, “Tang Aoqing Scholarship” and so on. He also host a number of basic or major research projects as a backbone.
